About my animal family

Our house can be a zoo at times because we have a dog and two cats. People always ask if they all get along and they do! They love each other! Flash used to mess with Snap and try to play with him but now he doesn't bother him. They're like a little pack. At first snap didn't like Doja but now they are best friends. They like laying by the window and cleaning each other.

If we were to get another animal, I would get another dog. Flash loves other dogs and has a lot of energy. I would love to get him a brother or a sister. We need to wait until we get into a house with a fenced in backyard before we get another dog though. Hopefully we can move and get him a brother/sister soon! My favorite breed of dog is a King Charles Caveliere Spaniel. So I would love to get one of those dogs!

Fact: Another name for Doja's fur pattern is "Magpie"

Snap

Snap is a 2 year old tabby cat. He is the first cat I've ever had. He loves to be pet underneath his chin and to play with my hair. He is sweetest and quirkiest kitty I've ever met.

Flash

Flash is a whippet which is a medium sized greyhound. When we got him from the humane society he was shaking in a corner but now he is a really happy dog. He is a very nervous dog so I try my best to make him feel protected.

Doja

Doja is a 5 month old tuxito kitty. She loves aggressively rubbing against you so you will pet her. She is very vocal and will let you know when she wants something. She's the only other girl of the family.
